Experience the Power of Neural Networks

  • The project utilizes a custom Mask Detection model trained on Tensoflow 2.0 and OpenCV's Face detection Neural Network model
  • The custom Mask detection model was trained using the Transfer learning technique which was ideal for training this model on a smaller dataset
  • The Trained Model has a Training accuracy of 99.44% and Validation accuracy of 98.69%

Dataset

The dataset consists of 1,376 images belonging to two classes:

  • with_mask: 690 images
  • without_mask: 686 images

Mask detection

Rectangular bounding boxes are generated for the detected faces where the color of box indicates the presence of the mask

  • with mask:Green
  • without mask:Red

Multiple Faces Classification

The faces detected in image are iterated and passed to the Mask prediction model individually

The Mask confidence percentage is also displayed on top of the bounding box
